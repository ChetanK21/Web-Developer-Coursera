Pasta

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Burger

A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, placed inside a sliced bread roll. The patty may be pan fried, grilled, smoked or flame broiled.

Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with various ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature.
